Jeff Eckhaus is the Vice President of Services and Infrastructure for Cobalt Talon. Jeff has spent 19 years in data management, business intelligence, and analytics. Prior to joining Cobalt Talon, Jeff was a Vice President at Praeos Technologies, a data management and business intelligence consulting firm. Jeff began his data management career in the Data Warehouse practice of PriceWaterhouseCoopers. Jeff has led numerous client engagements involving strategies, methodologies, best practices, and technical implementations of data and analytical solutions. His clients include several of the Fortune 100 companies, as well as many smaller companies.

Jeff has a Computer Science degree from The Pennsylvania State University.
